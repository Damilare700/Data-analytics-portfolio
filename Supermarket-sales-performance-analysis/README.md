# Supermarket Sales Performance Analysis

### **Project Overview**
Built executive dashboards in **Power BI and Excel** analyzing $6.63B in international sales data to identify top-performing regions, products, and channels. Designed for leadership to monitor KPIs, spot revenue risks, and guide strategic decisions.

### **Scope**
Analyzed 5,000+ transactions from 2015–2022 covering 7 global regions and 12 product categories. Tracked revenue, profit margins, and operational metrics including order fulfillment and cancellations.

### **Key Features**
**Power BI Version:**
- **DAX Measures**: Total Revenue, Gross Profit, Profit Margin %, Cancelled Order Value
- **Visuals**: Revenue by region map, product profitability bars, monthly trend line, channel donut
- **Interactivity**: Slicers for Region, Product Type, Sales Channel, Year, Order Priority
- **Drill-through**: Click region → see country and product breakdown

**Excel Version:**
- **Pivot Analysis**: Revenue by Month, Product Type, Region, Order Priority  
- **KPI Cards**: Total Revenue $6.63B, Top Region, Top Product, Cancelled Order Count
- **Charts**: Regional profit comparison, product performance ranking, channel split
- **Slicers**: Dynamic filtering for exec reviews

### **Key Insights**
1. **Revenue Leaders**: Sub-Saharan Africa $1.81B and Europe $1.70B accounted for 53% of total revenue
2. **Top Products**: Cosmetics delivered $372.4M profit, Household $347.9M, Office Supplies $266.1M
3. **Channel Parity**: Offline 2,504 orders vs Online 2,496 — near-equal distribution  
4. **Revenue Risk**: 1,174 cancelled orders represent $1.58B in lost revenue, 23% of order volume
5. **Peak Period**: January generated $652.6M, making Q1 the strongest quarter

### **Tools & Techniques**
Power BI, Excel, DAX, Power Query, Pivot Tables, Data Modeling, KPI Design, Dashboard Development
